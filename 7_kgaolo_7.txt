Kgaolo 7 --- Tshireletso Ya Rona E E Nosi

Batho ba Modimo ba lebisiwa kwa Baebeleng e le tshireletso ya bone kgatlhanong
le dinonofo tse di tsietsang tsa mewa ya lefifi. Satane o dirisa maano o tlhe
a a kaa kgonang go kganela batho go bona kitso ya Baebele. Ko tsosolosong nngwe
le nngwe ya tiro ya Modimo, o godisa tlhoafalo ya gagwe. Kgogakgogano ya
bofelo kgatlhanong le Keresete le balatedi ba Gagwe e gaufi le go phunyega fa
pele ga rona. Mo go etsang go tsile le go tshwana le ga boammaaruri mo e leng
gore go tlaa nna dingalo go difarologanya fa e se fela ka Baebele.

Ba ba lekang go obamela melao yotlhe ya Modimo ba tsile go engwa kgatlhanong le go
kgeswa. Go itshokela teko, ba tshwanetse go tlhaloganya go rata ga Modimo jaaka
go senotswe mo Lefokong la Gagwe. Ba ka mo tlotla fela fa ba tlhaloganya sentle
popego ya Gagwe, puso ya Gagwe, le maikaelelo a gagwe, le go tsiboga ka fa
tumalanong le tsone. Ga gona yo o tla kgonang go emelelela kgotlhang ya bofelo fa 
e se ba ba nonotshitsheng tlhaloganyo ya bone ka maammaaruri a Baebele.

Pele ga papolo ya Gagwe Mmoloki o ne a tlholosetsa barutwa ba Gagwe gore O ya
go bolawa mme O tlaa tsoga gape. Baengele ba ne ba le teng go gatelela mafoko a
Gagwe mo ditlhaloganyong le mo dipelong. Mme mafoko a a ne a nyelela mo
ditlhaloganyong tsa barutwa. Fa teko e tla, loso lwa ga Jesu lwa nyeletsa
ditsholofelo tsa bone jaaka e ka re o ne a sa ba tlhagisa go le pele. Jalo mo
diporofesong bokamoso bo butswe fa pele ga rona jaaka fela bone bo butswe
fa pele ga barutwa ke Keresete. Mme matshwitshwiti ga ana kitso ya mammaaruri
a jaaka e ka reng ga a ise a senolwe.

Fa Modimo a romela ditlhagiso, o lopa gore motho mongwe le mongwe yo o filweng
tlhaloganyo a reetse molaetsa. Dikatlholo tse di boifisang kgatlhanong le go
obamela sebatana le setshwantso sa sone (bona Tshenolo 14:9-11) di tshwanetse
go tsibosa botlhe go ithuta gore lotshwao lwa sebatana ke eng le gore lo ka
falolwa jang go lo amogela.* Mme bontsi jwa batho ga bo batle boammaaruri jwa
Baebele, ka go re bo kgoreletsa dikeletso tsa pelo ya boleo. Satane a ba a ba
fa ditsietso tse ba di ratang.

Mme le fa go ntse jalo Modimo o tlaa nna le batho ba ba tla tshegetsang
Baebele, Baebele e le yosi, e le yone seelo sa dithuto tsotlhe le tshimologo ya
dikago sesha tsotlhe. Maikutlo a barutegi, dikakanyetso tsa maranyane,
ditshwetso tsa diphutego tsa seruti, lentswe la bontsi---sepe sa tse ga se a
tshwanela go tsewa jaaka bosupedi jwa thuto ya tumelo kgotsa bosupi kgatlhanong
le yone. Re tshwanetse go batla "Go bua Morena" yo o senang bana ba phefo.
Satane o gogela batho ba ba ntsi go leba ko baruting, mo go ba dithutego tse di
tona mo tumelong jaaka bakaedi ba bone, go nale go ipalela Baebele ka bo bone.
Ka go laola baeteledipele ba, o ka laola matshwititshwiti.

Fa Keresete a tsile, batho botlhe ba ne ba mo utlwa ka boitumelo. Mme
baetelepele ba seperesita le bagogi bone ba itswalela tsheleng; ba gana bosupi
jwa Bogolodi jwa Gagwe. "Go tla jang," batho ba botsa, "gore babusi ba rona le
baruti ga ba dumele mo go Jesu?" Baruti ba go nna jalo ba gogela tshaba ya
Sejuta go gana Morekolodi wa bone.

Go Tsholetsa Taolo Ya Setho

Keresete o ne a bonela pele tiro ya go tsholetsa taolo ya setho go busa
segakolodi, se e nnileng phutso e kgolo mo dingwageng tsotlhe. Ditlhagiso tsa
Gagwe tsa gore go seka ga salwa morago baeteledipele ba ba sa boneng ya bewa mo
mokwalong e le kgakolotlhagiso ya dikokomane tse di tlang.

Kereke ya Roma e fa baruti ba le nosi tetla ya go tlhalosa Baebele. Le fa
poposesha e ne ya neela botlhe Baebele, tshetlha e e neng ya
tshegediwa ke Roma go kganela matshwititshwiti go iphukuletsa Baebele e ntse e
kganela ba ba mo dikerekeng tse e seng tsa Seroma. Ba rutwa go amogela dithuto
<i>jaaka di tlhaloswa ke kereke</i>. Dikete di be di sa batle go amogela sepe,
go sa kgathalesege gore Baebele e tlhamaletse jang, se se kgatlhanong le tumelo
ya bone.
